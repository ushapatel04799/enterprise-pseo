# PRODUCT NAME

Enterprise Autonomous Programmatic SEO Engine

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# PRODUCT VISION

Create the world's most intelligent
AI-powered Programmatic SEO Platform
for local businesses.

The platform should allow a business owner
to generate thousands of high-quality,
Google-friendly,
location-specific landing pages
from structured datasets
without manually writing content.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# MISSION

Help local businesses generate
qualified organic leads
through high-quality localized websites,
not thin programmatic pages.
