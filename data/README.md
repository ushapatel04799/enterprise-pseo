# Data

## Purpose

Provide the governed source-data home for configuration-driven PSEO content.

## Responsibilities

Organize approved niche, location, service, FAQ, and content-template datasets with stable contracts and provenance.

## What Belongs Here

- `niches/` — approved niche taxonomy and policy data.
- `locations/` — geographic hierarchy and eligibility data.
- `services/` — service definitions and approved attributes.
- `faqs/` — approved question-and-answer source material.
- `templates/` — structured content-template data, not presentation code.

## What Must Never Be Placed Here

Secrets, credentials, unverified facts, runtime caches, handwritten generated pages, application code, or mutable production-only data.
